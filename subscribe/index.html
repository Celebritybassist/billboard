Follow every beat of the music business

Money’s changing everything in the music business. As streaming revenue grows, labels are bidding for hot acts, private equity players are buying up publishing catalogs, and online platforms are paying more than ever for the rights their businesses depend on. Billboard Pro breaks down the business, with incisive reporting, insightful analysis, and enlightening interviews with the artists and executives behind these changes.
